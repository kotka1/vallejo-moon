## One line

Independent local journalism for Vallejo and Solano County — clear, accountable, and rooted in the community.

## What we cover

- **Government** — city council, budgets, development
- **Community** — housing, education, labor, small business
- **Public safety** — policing, accountability (reporting, not cheerleading)
- **Culture** — arts, music, events, neighborhoods
- **En español** — when stories serve Spanish-speaking readers (follow Vallejo Sun’s model of bilingual coverage)

## What we are not

- A PR arm for politicians or developers
- Clickbait aggregation
- Anonymous hit pieces — we stand behind reported work

## Voice

- Direct, local, human
- Lead with **who / what / where / when / why**
- Link to primary sources (agendas, police logs, court filings) when possible

## Comparison note (internal)

[The Vallejo Sun](https://www.vallejosun.com/) has established beats: government, policing, housing, education, arts. Vallejo Moon should find a **distinct angle** — geographic focus, format (evening digest, investigative depth, youth voices, multimedia), or underserved neighborhoods — rather than duplicating coverage blindly.
