# Style guide (draft)

## Headlines

- Sentence case for web: `Vallejo council votes on pool closure`
- Active voice; specific place names

## Article structure

```markdown
---
title: ""
slug: ""
author: ""
date: YYYY-MM-DD
category: government | policing | housing | education | arts | business | events
status: draft | review | published
---

Lead paragraph — the news in 1–2 sentences.

## Section

Body copy…
```

## Categories (match site nav)

`government` · `policing` · `housing` · `education` · `arts` · `business` · `events` · `labor` · `crime`

## Names

- First reference: full name + role if relevant
- **Vallejo** not “the city of Vallejo” every time

## Photos

- Credit line required: `Photo: Name / Vallejo Moon`
- Store originals in `content/media/` (create when needed)

## Ethics

- Correct errors publicly
- Disclose conflicts of interest
- Minimize harm — thoughtful coverage of crime and trauma
